# Kenya National Health Framework

## Malaria Control Strategy
In Kenya, malaria remains a leading cause of morbidity and mortality, particularly in the Lake endemic regions (such as Kisumu, Homa Bay, and Siaya) and coastal areas.

### Management Protocols
- The Ministry of Health guides that all fever cases presenting at clinics must be tested using malaria RDTs.
- Confirmed uncomplicated malaria cases are treated strictly with Artemisinin-based Combination Therapies (ACTs).
- Kenya has actively integrated the RTS,S/AS01 malaria vaccine into routine immunization schedules for children in high-risk lake-endemic counties to decrease severe disease rates.
