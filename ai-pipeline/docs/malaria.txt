Malaria is caused by parasites transmitted through mosquito bites.

Symptoms include:
- fever
- chills
- headache
- vomiting
- sweating
- fatigue

Prevention:
- sleep under mosquito nets
- use mosquito repellent
- avoid stagnant water

Treatment:
- antimalarial drugs
- seek medical attention early
